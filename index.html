Current Progress

My name is Mohammad Alabed, and I am currently studying web development. I've always have a deep interest in programming, but it mostly consisted of seeing the end-result. Being purely honest, web-development is probably the field I genuinely hate the most. It feels that mistakes in it are countless and inevitable, that even the simplest mistakes take far too long to fix unlike conventional programming, and the end-result doesn't have the same "omph" as it does for programming. Nonetheless, I self-studied it a bit roughly a year ago and gave up before finishing the ideal website I had in mind. The process was excrutiating, and something I'll likely never live down - and being fair, it was mostly due to how I approached learning it with the most ineffective way I could've, pretty much just winging it. But, I decided to give it another chance by taking this course and attempting to start from scratch. As you can see with the image, I didn't entirely have the best start, but I guess it's a start nonetheless.
